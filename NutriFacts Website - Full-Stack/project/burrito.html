Burrito Bowl

Learn how to make a delicious and nutritious burrito bowl with this easy recipe.

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 20 minutes
  • Total Time: 35 minutes
  • Yields: 2 servings

Protein Levels: Each serving of Burrito Bowl contains approximately 20 grams of protein.

Advantages of Burrito Bowl:

  • Provides a balanced combination of protein, carbohydrates, and healthy fats.
  • Packed with fiber from beans, vegetables, and whole grains.
  • Offers essential vitamins and minerals from various ingredients.
  • Customizable with your favorite toppings and ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • 1 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup grilled chicken, sliced
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup red onion, diced
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: avocado, Greek yogurt, shredded cheese

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked brown rice, black beans, grilled chicken, corn kernels, cherry tomatoes, red onion, bell peppers, and fresh cilantro.
  2. Squeeze the lime juice over the mixture and season with salt and pepper to taste. Toss well to combine all the ingredients.
  3. Divide the burrito bowl mixture into serving bowls.
  4. Top with optional toppings such as avocado slices, a dollop of Greek yogurt, and shredded cheese.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Enjoy!

Indulge in this flavorful and nutritious Burrito Bowl. Customize it with your favorite toppings and enjoy a satisfying meal.
