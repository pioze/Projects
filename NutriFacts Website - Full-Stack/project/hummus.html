Peanut Butter Hummus

Learn how to make delicious and creamy Peanut Butter Hummus using simple ingredients.

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 0 minutes
  • Total Time: 10 minutes
  • Yields: Approximately 1 cup

Protein Levels: Each serving of Peanut Butter Hummus contains approximately 6 grams of protein.

Advantages of Peanut Butter Hummus:

  • A great source of plant-based protein.
  • Peanut butter adds a rich and creamy texture to the hummus.
  • Packed with essential nutrients like healthy fats, fiber, and vitamins.
  • Can be enjoyed as a dip, spread, or filling for sandwiches and wraps.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 ounces) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 cup smooth peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2-4 tablespoons water (adjust for desired consistency)
  • Optional toppings: chopped peanuts, drizzle of olive oil, paprika

Instructions

  1. In a food processor or blender, combine the drained chickpeas, peanut butter, lemon juice, olive oil, minced garlic, ground cumin, and salt.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy. If the mixture is too thick, add water, one tablespoon at a time, until desired consistency is reached.
  3. Transfer the peanut butter hummus to a serving bowl.
  4. Optional: Garnish with chopped peanuts, a drizzle of olive oil, and a sprinkle of paprika.
  5. Serve with your favorite vegetables, pita bread, or crackers.

Enjoy!

Indulge in the creamy and flavorful Peanut Butter Hummus. It's a delightful twist on traditional hummus that you'll love.
