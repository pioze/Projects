Oatmeal Pecan Waffles

Learn how to make delicious and wholesome Oatmeal Pecan Waffles with a hint of maple flavor.

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 15 minutes
  • Total Time: 25 minutes
  • Yields: 4 waffles

Protein Levels: Each serving of Oatmeal Pecan Waffles contains approximately 8 grams of protein.

Advantages of Oatmeal Pecan Waffles:

  • Rich in fiber and nutrients from oats and pecans.
  • Provides sustained energy and helps you feel full for longer.
  • A great way to start your day with a delicious and nutritious breakfast.
  • Can be customized with your favorite toppings like fresh fruits, yogurt, or maple syrup.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup whole wheat flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cups milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • 1/4 cup pure maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup chopped pecans
  • Optional toppings: fresh fruits, yogurt, maple syrup

Instructions

  1. In a blender or food processor, blend the rolled oats until finely ground.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the ground oats, whole wheat flour, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, maple syrup, and vanilla extract.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Do not overmix.
  5. Gently fold in the chopped pecans.
  6. Preheat a waffle iron according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  7. Lightly grease the waffle iron with cooking spray or oil.
  8. Scoop the waffle batter onto the preheated waffle iron, using about 1/2 cup of batter per waffle.
  9. Cook the waffles according to the manufacturer's instructions, until golden brown and crispy.
  10. Remove the waffles from the iron and keep them warm in a preheated oven while cooking the remaining batter.
  11. Transfer the cooked waffles to serving plates.
  12. Optional: Serve with your favorite toppings such as fresh fruits, yogurt, or a drizzle of maple syrup.

Enjoy!

Delight in the wholesome and satisfying Oatmeal Pecan Waffles. They are perfect for a nourishing breakfast or brunch.
