Empanadas Verdes

Learn how to make delicious Empanadas Verdes filled with green vegetables and cheese.

  • Prep Time: 30 minutes
  • Cook Time: 25 minutes
  • Total Time: 55 minutes
  • Yields: 12 empanadas

Protein Levels: Each serving of Empanadas Verdes contains approximately 10 grams of protein.

Advantages of Empanadas Verdes:

  • Packed with green vegetables that provide essential vitamins and minerals.
  • Rich in fiber from the whole wheat dough.
  • A delicious and healthy snack or meal option.
  • Can be customized with additional ingredients or fillings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 cup spinach leaves, blanched and chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/2 cup green onions, chopped
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese (such as mozzarella or cheddar)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the whole wheat flour and salt. Gradually add the cold water and olive oil, mixing until a smooth dough forms.
  2. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  3. In the meantime, prepare the filling. In a separate bowl, combine the chopped spinach, parsley, cilantro, green onions, grated cheese, cumin powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  5. Remove the dough from the refrigerator and divide it into 12 equal portions.
  6. On a lightly floured surface, roll out each portion of dough into a circle approximately 6 inches in diameter.
  7. Place a spoonful of the filling in the center of each dough circle. Fold the dough over the filling, pressing the edges together to seal. You can use a fork to create a decorative pattern on the edges.
  8. Transfer the filled empanadas to the prepared baking sheet.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until the empanadas are golden brown.
  10. Remove from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving.

Enjoy!

Savor the delightful flavors of Empanadas Verdes. These green vegetable-filled empanadas are perfect for any occasion or as a tasty snack.
